Good morning everyone. Today, I am going to talk about a very important work project we recently carried out at my company. The project aims to improve delivery times and increase customer satisfaction.

To give you some context, before we started this project, the company **had struggled** with constant shipping delays. We had lost several important contracts **because** our inventory system was completely obsolete. **Therefore**, the management decided that we needed an urgent update.

The implementation process was challenging but rewarding. We **started** the research phase last year. Then, we **trained** the employees in January, and our team finally **launched** the new platform in March.

So far, the results are excellent. We **have reduced** operational costs by twenty percent. Furthermore, the development team **has implemented** key changes in the warehouse interface. **Although** we faced some initial bugs, we have fixed them very quickly.

Looking towards the future, we have great expectations. **If we keep** updating the software regularly, the project **will save** us a lot of money next year. **If we meet** the final deadline next month, the company **will give** a bonus to all the staff members.

**However**, it is also important to reflect on the process. For example, **if we had** more engineers on the team right now, we **would finish** the mobile version much faster. Also, **if we had started** earlier, we **would have finished** by now.

In conclusion, this project taught us the value of innovation and teamwork. Thank you very much for your attention.
