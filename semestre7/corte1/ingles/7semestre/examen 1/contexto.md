IDENTIFYING:

    Students will identify the correct use of Present Perfect and Past Simple in context.

    Students will differentiate between affirmative, negative and interrogative forms of both tenses.

    Students will recognize and use time expressions such as yesterday, last week, recently, in 2010 accurately.

    Students will distinguish regular and irregular verb forms (e.g., run, walked, eats, have seen) in each tense.

    Students will identify linking words (because, although, therefore, however) and their function in connecting ideas.
