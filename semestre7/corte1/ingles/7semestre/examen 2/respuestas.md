### Pregunta 3: What does the sentence "If we had turned on the GPS, we wouldn't be stuck…" express?

> This is a Third Conditional structure that expresses regret or criticism about what actually happened in the past.

> "if i were alone i would've canceled this trip already"

## PREGUNTA 11: MATCHING - COINCIDENCIA

**Match the meaning with the corresponding word." (1 punto)**

| Oración / Pregunta | Respuesta Correcta | Tipo de Condicional |
|----------|-------------------|-------------------|
| If I won the lottery | I would buy a new car. | Second Conditional |
| If I were the president | I would improve education. | Second Conditional |
| They won't come | If we don't invite them. | First Conditional (causa-efecto) |
| She will arrive on time | If she catches the early train. | First Conditional (causa-efecto) |
| We will miss the bus | If we don't leave now. | First Conditional (causa-efecto) |
| If he knew the answer | he would raise his hand. | Second Conditional |
| You will get wet | If you forget your umbrella. | First Conditional (causa-efecto) |
| If I had a dog | I would take it for a walk every day. | Second Conditional |

### Explicación:

- **Second Conditional** (Si + pasado = consecuencia hipotética presente): "If I won...", "If I were...", "If he knew..." → expresan situaciones imaginarias o poco probables
- **First Conditional** (Si + presente = consecuencia futura real): Las afirmaciones simples se conectan con sus condiciones

## PREGUNTAS 12-21: READING COMPREHENSION - TRUE OR FALSE

**Basado en el texto: "Group Project Challenges"**

### Pregunta 12: Julia joined a new study group for her English class.
**Respuesta: TRUE** ✓

> "Last semester, Julia joined a new study group for her English class."

---

### Pregunta 13: If someone turned on their camera, the others would usually stay quiet.
**Respuesta: TRUE** ✓

> "if someone turned on their camera during meetings, the others would often stay quiet. It made communication difficult."

---

### Pregunta 14: Julia created the calendar by herself without asking anyone.
**Respuesta: FALSE** ✗

> "She suggested creating a calendar and dividing tasks clearly." - Ella sugirió la idea, no la hizo sola.

---

### Pregunta 15: If the group follows Julia's plan, they will finish on time.
**Respuesta: TRUE** ✓

> "If we follow this plan," she said, "we will finish on time."

---

### Pregunta 16: The team passed the project in the end.
**Respuesta: TRUE** ✓

> "It wasn't a perfect project, but they passed—and learned a lot about teamwork."

---

### Pregunta 17: If the group had communicated better from the beginning, they would have avoided stress.
**Respuesta: TRUE** ✓

> "if we had been more organized from the beginning, we wouldn't have stressed so much." (La comunicación y organización son conceptos relacionados)

---

### Pregunta 18: Julia thinks that if she had worked alone, the result would have been better.
**Respuesta: FALSE** ✗

> El texto no sugiere esto. De hecho, Julia aprendió la importancia de la comunicación y el trabajo en equipo.

---

### Pregunta 19: If another team had the same problems, they would benefit from using Julia's strategy.
**Respuesta: TRUE** ✓

> La estrategia de Julia (crear un calendario y dividir tareas) fue exitosa y resolvió los problemas del grupo.

---

### Pregunta 20: Julia learned that she should wait before making any suggestions.
**Respuesta: FALSE** ✗

> Julia hizo sugerencias útiles (crear calendario, dividir tareas). El texto no dice que deba esperar para hacer sugerencias.

---

### Pregunta 21: If Julia works with another team in the future, she will set expectations early.
**Respuesta: TRUE** ✓

> "If I work with another team in the future," she told herself, "I won't put off setting expectations early."
